What we help with

Concrete financial work, not vague advice.

We do the analytical work a CFO would do — and deliver it to you in plain language you can act on.

01

Monthly MIS & management reporting

Actuals vs budget, variance commentary, and trend analysis — written up clearly, ready for your board or investors.

→ So you always know what changed, why it changed, and what to do next.
02

Budgeting & forecasting

Rolling 12-month forecasts, scenario models (base/upside/downside), and driver-based budgets that reflect your business.

→ So you can plan hiring, spending, and fundraising with real numbers behind it.
03

Cash flow visibility

30/60/90-day cash projections. Know when you'll be short, when you can invest, and how long your runway really is.

→ So you know exactly when you'll run out of cash — before it happens.
04

Variance & profitability analysis

Line-by-line variance explanations, root cause identification, and margin analysis by product, client, or channel.

→ So you stop wondering why profit doesn't match revenue growth.
05

Revenue & cost insight

Understand where your money actually goes. We break down fixed vs variable, productive vs wasteful, and show you the levers.

→ So you cut the right costs and protect the ones that drive growth.
06

Finance dashboards for decision-making

Clean, visual dashboards that answer the questions you actually ask — not 50 charts nobody reads.

→ So every leadership meeting starts with clarity, not confusion.
